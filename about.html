About Us

SONLIGHT is an Information Technology (IT) company based in Batangas City, Philippines that aims to bring services that bridges light and sounds companies and their customers together. At SONLIGHT, the main aim is to provide convenience into booking and searching for the best fitting sound and light services to the customers and to also bring convenience in handling booking agreements for the owners. SONLIGHT strives to provide exceptional customer service and convinience to the light and sound companies and their customers.

Mission

To connect individuals with reliable light and sound rental store, with simplified process and enhanced accesibility.

Vision

To be the leading online booking platform for light and sound equipment rentals, creating a trusted community that builds strong relationships with users.
